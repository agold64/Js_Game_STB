Game Rules

After rolling, add up the dice and then flip one of any combination of open numbers that equals the total number on the dice.

For example, if the total number is 8, the player may choose any of the following sets of numbers (as long as all of the numbers in the set are available to be covered):

  • 8
  • 7, 1
  • 6, 2
  • 5, 3
  • 5, 2, 1
  • 4, 3, 1
